1. After roll-call no Hosteler is allowed to leave the hostel but if an emergency arises, she must inform the Warden.

2. Hostelers can meet the Visitors (No male allowed inside the hostel premises) between 8:00 am to 6:00pm.

3. Visitors to the hostel inmates will be required to enter their names and address and the name of the student whom they want to see etc. in the register
kept with the duty security guard at the Institute entrance.

4. Check-in and check-out, should be properly entered in the Register kept by the Warden.

5. No Hosteler will be allowed to enter in the hostel after 6:00 P.M unless she is permitted by the authority to do so for specific reasons.

6. In case of an emergency arises a hosteler can leave hostel with the written permission of the Hostel- in- charge, with the consent and physical presence
of the Parent or Guardian.

7. Those who are normally leaving hostel, are required to apply for leave, to the warden, in the prescribed form, in advance and such an application must be
counter signed by the warden.

8. If any Hosteler is found missing from the hostel without the permission from the Warden/ Resident Warden, she will be severely dealt with disciplinary action,
financial penalty and expulsion from the hostel

9. Using of narcotic drugs, consumption of alcoholic beverages, smoking, betting, playing of cards and gambling are strictly prohibited.

10. Firearms, knives or such other weapons are strictly prohibited in the Hostel premises.

11. Ragging in any form is strictly prohibited. It constitutes an act of gross indiscipline and any person found involved in such an activity will be subjected
to appropriate action from heavy fine to expulsion from the college.

12. It shall be ensured by all Hostelers that they do not get involved in fighting among themselves, peer or with juniors. In case such incidents are brought to the
management, appropriate disciplinary action will be taken against such students.

13. No hosteler should get involved in any argument/ heated exchanges with the security and supporting staff. In case of any complaint against them, they should register
the same in the register kept with Warden for this purpose giving their full details and identity.

14. Use of audio equipment/music systems in hostels is acceptable only if it is not objectionable to other residents.

15. Any hostel rooms can be inspected by the authorities at any time.
